BACKGROUND

Modomelane Trading and Projects (PTY) LTD was established in 2014 It provide itself in not only what has been delivered, but also in the way how it’s delivered with the emphasis on quality and professionalism.

Modomelane Trading and Projects (PTY) LTD is divided into 2 departments, logistics and civil construction, our logistics department does hauling of coal from the mines to eskom power stations, our construction department is focused on building & civil construction of top class high end projects, we also offer project management, quantity surveying, and maintenance.

In accordance with the notice of incorporation, the registration of the company took effect on 08/04/2014. In conjunction with this certificate, the commission has not issued another notice contemplated in section 14(2)(b).
